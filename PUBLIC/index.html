Receipt Splitter

This app helps you categorize expenses from a single receipt. Let's say you went to Target and spent $100 on some groceries, some toiletries and some baby supplies. Now you want to enter your expenses into your monthly budget categories, but you aren't sure how much of that $100 you spent in each category.

Here's how you'd enter that information into the app:

  1. Enter the grand total and the amount of tax from the receipt in "Receipt Info"
  2. Edit the name of each category you want to use. You can add and delete categories, too!
  3. Go through your receipt and enter each expense in the corresponding category. The app will total up each category and keep track of the grand total in the Receipt Info box

(This is a work in progress - not all functions work as advertised yet)
